CloudAssist is a research prototype from the SUPERgroup at University of Chicago designed to study how users of cloud storage services understand, organize and manage their files. To do this, it scans the files in your Google Drive, and uses this information to identify which files in your account are likely to be similar to each other. It then offers recommendations of file actions you might find useful. It does this in a custom file management interface that we host in this same application. For more information on why we request particular permissions for your Google Drive account, and how we handle your data, you may read our Privacy Policy.

For this study, we ask your permission to scan your Google Drive account in order to find groups of files we believe would be recommended together. We then offer explanations for this behavior, and ask your perceptions of these explanations.
